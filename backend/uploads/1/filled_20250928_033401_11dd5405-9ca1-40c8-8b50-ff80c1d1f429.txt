The greenhouse effect occurs when gases like CO2 and methane trap outgoing infrared radiation, warming the lower atmosphere and surface. Sunlight enters easily, but heat is partially retained, creating a natural blanket that keeps average temperatures livable.

A citywide building retrofit program can fund heat-pump installations and insulation upgrades in older homes. Pair financing with contractor training and tiered rebates for low-income households.
