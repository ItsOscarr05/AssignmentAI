Q1) Name two factors that influence climate in coastal regions.  
A1) Proximity to the ocean and prevailing winds.

Q2) 30–50 words: Explain why mountain ranges affect rainfall patterns.  
A2) Mountain ranges influence rainfall patterns through orographic lift, where moist air rises, cools, and condenses on the windward side, causing precipitation. The leeward side experiences rain shadow effects, resulting in drier conditions.
